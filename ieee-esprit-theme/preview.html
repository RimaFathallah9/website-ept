About Us

IEEE EPT Student Branch is one of the newest Student Branches in Tunisia. Founded in 2025, IEEE EPT Student Branch made its way to do more than 30 events this year and include more than 50 members in less than one year (50%).

It includes 3 technical chapters (CS, SMCS) alongside one affinity group (WIE). We are dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.
